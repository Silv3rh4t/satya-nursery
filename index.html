Plant Parenting 101

Expert tips to keep your green companions thriving

Watering Wisdom

Most plants die from overwatering, not underwatering. Check the soil moisture by sticking your finger 2 inches deep. Water only when the top layer feels dry.

Best time: Early morning

Light Requirements

Understand your light conditions. South-facing windows get the most light, while north-facing are coolest. Rotate plants weekly for even growth and prevent leaning.

Rotate weekly

Soil & Repotting

Choose well-draining soil mixes. Repot when roots start growing out of drainage holes or circle the pot. Spring is the best season for repotting most plants.

Spring: Best season

Pruning & Cleaning

Regular pruning encourages bushier growth. Remove dead or yellowing leaves to redirect energy. Wipe leaves monthly to remove dust and help with photosynthesis.

Monthly cleaning

Pest Management

Inspect leaves regularly for pests. Neem oil is a natural solution for most common insects. Quarantine new plants for 2 weeks before placing them near others.

Neem oil solution

Fertilizing

Feed plants during growing season (spring/summer) with balanced liquid fertilizer. Reduce feeding in fall and winter when plant growth naturally slows down.

Spring-Summer only
